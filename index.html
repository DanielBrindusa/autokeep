AutoKeep Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AutoKeep handles local records, optional online account features, imported files, and permanent account deletion.

Last updated: April 13, 2026

1. Who this policy applies to

This Privacy Policy applies to users of the AutoKeep Android application with package name com.autokeep.app.

2. What AutoKeep does

AutoKeep helps users manage vehicle-related information in one organized place.

  • vehicle profiles
  • vehicle documents
  • reminders and due dates
  • proof files and notes
  • official links and public resources
  • optional online sync for selected private records
  • permanent online account and data deletion

3. Local-only use

You can use AutoKeep locally on your device without creating an online account.

  • private records can stay only on your device
  • shared official links may still be loaded online
  • private sync does not happen unless you choose to enable it

4. Information you may enter into the app

Depending on how you use AutoKeep, the app may process information such as:

  • vehicle profile details
  • document details
  • reminders and dates
  • notes and reference details
  • imported files, PDFs, images, and scans
  • email address for optional online account access
  • selected sync preferences

5. Optional online account and sync

AutoKeep offers an optional online account and optional online sync.

  • selected private data may be stored in the cloud database if you enable sync
  • selected uploaded files may be stored in online storage if sync requires them
  • only the categories you choose in the sync scope are intended to be uploaded
  • shared public official resources are separate from your private account data

6. Shared public resources

AutoKeep may show public official resources such as provider pages, approved public links, and country references. These public resources are not part of your private account data.

7. File imports and document handling

If you import files into AutoKeep, the app may copy selected files into app-managed private storage on your device so they can be attached to your records.

  • PDF documents
  • images
  • scanned files

8. Sign-in and account verification

If you create an online account, AutoKeep may process:

  • your email address
  • your password
  • email verification state

This is used to create your online account, sign you in, verify your email address, and protect access to synced private records.

9. How your information is used

  • display and organize your records
  • save private records on your device
  • sync selected records with your online account if you enable sync
  • show reminders and due-date information
  • provide access to public official resources
  • process sign-in, verification, and deletion requests
  • support backup, restore, export, and internal app functionality

10. Data deletion and account deletion

If you use an online account, AutoKeep provides a flow to permanently delete your online account and related online data.

  • only your own online account and related online data are targeted
  • your online account is permanently deleted
  • your synced records linked to that account are permanently deleted
  • your uploaded online files linked to that account are permanently deleted
  • app-owned sync metadata and related online records linked to that account are intended to be permanently deleted

Shared public resources are not part of your personal account deletion. After permanent deletion completes, the online account and synced data cannot be restored.

11. Your choices

  • use AutoKeep locally without an online account
  • decide which private categories are synced
  • stop using online sync
  • permanently delete your online account and related online data

12. Data security

AutoKeep is designed to handle private records carefully and to use secure account and deletion flows for optional online features.

No method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be perfectly secure, but AutoKeep is intended to use reasonable technical measures appropriate to the app’s purpose.

13. Children

AutoKeep is not designed specifically for children.

14. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If it changes, the updated version should be published with a new “Last updated” date.
