# AutoKeep Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

AutoKeep is an Android app for managing vehicle records such as documents, reminders, proofs, official links, and optional online sync settings.

This Privacy Policy explains what information AutoKeep may process, how the app uses that information, and what choices you have.

## 1. Who this policy applies to

This Privacy Policy applies to users of the AutoKeep Android application with package name `com.autokeep.app`.

## 2. What AutoKeep does

AutoKeep helps users:

- manage vehicle profiles
- store and review vehicle-related documents
- create reminders for important dates and expirations
- access public official links and provider resources
- optionally create an online account and sync selected private records
- permanently delete an online account and related online data

## 3. Local-only use

You can use AutoKeep locally on your device without creating an online account.

When you use AutoKeep in local-only mode:

- your private records are stored only on your device
- shared public official links may still be loaded online
- no private sync to your online account happens unless you choose to enable it

## 4. Information you may enter into the app

Depending on how you use AutoKeep, the app may process information such as:

- vehicle profile details
- vehicle document details
- reminders and due dates
- notes and reference information
- imported files, images, PDFs, and scans
- email address for online account sign-in
- selected sync preferences

## 5. Online account and optional sync

AutoKeep offers an optional online account and optional online sync.

If you choose to create an online account and enable sync:

- selected private data may be stored in the cloud database
- selected uploaded files may be stored in online storage
- only the data categories you choose in the sync scope are intended to be uploaded
- shared public official resources are separate from your private account data

Before account creation, AutoKeep shows a required notice explaining:

- that online sync is optional
- that local-only use remains available
- that selected private data may be stored online if sync is enabled
- that you can later permanently delete your online account and related online data

## 6. Shared public resources

AutoKeep may show public official resources such as:

- official links
- provider references
- country references

These public resources are not part of your private account data.

## 7. File imports and document handling

If you import files into AutoKeep, the app may copy selected files into app-managed private storage on your device so they can be attached to your records.

Examples include:

- PDF documents
- images
- scanned files

AutoKeep is designed to prefer privacy-friendly document and file handling patterns where possible.

## 8. Sign-in and account verification

If you create an online account, AutoKeep may process:

- your email address
- your password
- email verification state

This is used to:

- create your online account
- sign you in
- verify your email address
- protect access to synced private records

## 9. How your information is used

AutoKeep may use information you provide to:

- display and organize your records
- save your private records on your device
- sync selected records with your online account if you enable sync
- show reminders and due-date information
- provide access to public official resources
- process account sign-in, verification, and deletion requests
- support backup, restore, export, and internal app functionality

## 10. Data deletion and account deletion

If you use an online account, AutoKeep provides a flow to permanently delete your online account and related online data.

When you request permanent deletion:

- only your own online account and related online data are targeted
- your online account is permanently deleted
- your app-owned synced records linked to that account are permanently deleted
- your uploaded online files linked to that account are permanently deleted
- app-owned sync metadata and related backend records linked to that account are intended to be permanently deleted

Shared public resources are not part of your personal account deletion.

After permanent deletion completes:

- the online account cannot be restored
- online synced data cannot be restored

Local data on your device may also be removed according to the delete flow shown in the app.

## 11. Your choices

You can choose to:

- use AutoKeep locally without an online account
- decide which private categories are synced
- stop using online sync
- permanently delete your online account and related online data

## 12. Data security

AutoKeep is designed to handle private records carefully and to use secure account and deletion flows for optional online features.

No method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be perfectly secure, but AutoKeep is intended to use reasonable technical measures appropriate to the app’s purpose.

## 13. Children

AutoKeep is not designed specifically for children.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

If it changes, the updated version should be published with a new “Last updated” date.
