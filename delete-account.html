AutoKeep

Delete Online Account and Data

This page explains how AutoKeep users can permanently delete their online account and related online data.

App: AutoKeep

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Support contact: brindusa.daniel@gmail.com

1. Delete directly inside the app

If you are signed in to your AutoKeep online account, open the app and go to:

  1. Settings
  2. Permanent deletion options
  3. Choose one of the two deletion options
  4. Re-authenticate and confirm the deletion

2. The two deletion choices

  • Delete online account and online data permanently: deletes the online account and related online data, but keeps the local AutoKeep data saved on the device.
  • Delete online account, online data, and local device data permanently: deletes the online account and related online data, and also deletes the private AutoKeep data saved on the device.

3. What is deleted from the online account

When an online deletion request is completed, AutoKeep is intended to permanently delete the requesting user’s online account and user-owned online data, including:

  • the online account used for AutoKeep sign-in
  • vehicles saved in the online account
  • documents saved in the online account
  • document file metadata saved in the online account
  • reminders saved in the online account
  • uploaded files stored for the user under the user-owned storage path
  • sync metadata and account-related online metadata tied to that user

Shared public resources such as official links are not part of user account deletion.

4. What stays on the phone for the online-only option

If the user chooses Delete online account and online data permanently, the app is intended to:

  • delete the online account and related online data
  • sign the user out
  • return the app to local-only mode
  • keep the private AutoKeep data already saved on the phone

5. What is also deleted for the full deletion option

If the user chooses Delete online account, online data, and local device data permanently, the app is intended to:

  • delete the online account and related online data
  • delete the private AutoKeep data saved on the phone
  • return the app to a fresh local-empty state

6. If you cannot access the app

If you cannot access the app and need help with account deletion, contact: brindusa.daniel@gmail.com

Include the email address of the AutoKeep online account you want reviewed.
