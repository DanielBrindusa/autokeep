AutoKeep Account Deletion

This page explains how AutoKeep users can permanently delete their online account and related online data.

Last updated: April 13, 2026

1. What this page is for

AutoKeep users who created an online account can request permanent deletion of that online account and related online data.

2. How to delete your account inside the app

Use the in-app deletion flow:

  1. Open AutoKeep.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Open Delete account and all data permanently.
  4. Enter your account email and password.
  5. Type DELETE to confirm.
  6. Confirm the warning checkbox.
  7. Tap Delete account and all data permanently.

3. What is deleted

When permanent deletion completes, AutoKeep is intended to delete the following for that user:

  • online account access
  • private synced records linked to that account
  • uploaded online files linked to that account
  • sync metadata and app-owned online records linked to that account

4. What is not part of account deletion

The following are not part of personal account deletion:

  • shared public official links
  • shared provider references
  • shared country references
  • other users and their data

5. Local device data

Depending on the in-app flow, local app data on the device may also be wiped as part of permanent deletion after the online deletion completes successfully.

6. Important warning

Permanent deletion cannot be undone. After the online account and related online data are deleted, they cannot be restored.
